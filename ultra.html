The Ultramarines

Headed by the primarch Roboute Guilliman the Ultramines are strict and honor bound warriors who live by the Codex Astartes. Hailing from the homeworld of Macragge the eighth chapter is one of the most widely respected and honored chapters. Since the second founding they have maintained some of the purest gene seed in the Adeptus Astartes. They follow the Codex Astartes, a blueprint of the holy ideals on which the Ultramarines plan, train and fight by.
