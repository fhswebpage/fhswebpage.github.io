The Imperial Records

Greetings Librarian, this is the official records location of the Adeptus Astartes as instated by the Departmeno Munitorum. Located here are the histories of each chapter updated as of (DATA EXPUNGED). The available chapters are the Ultramarines of Ultramar, The Imperial Fists of the Phanalx, the Black Templars of the Crusade Fleet, and the Blood Angels of Aurelia. To navigate towards the chapter texts locate the corresponding insignia. If you wish to return to this index, click the Adeptus Mechanicus skull insignia.

If you have a malfuction or suspected corruption of text contact the nearest tech preist.
